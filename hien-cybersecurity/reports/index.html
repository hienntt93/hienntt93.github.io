Security Reports & Deliverables

What you can expect from every engagement.

Report contents

Professional deliverables included in every engagement.

  • Executive summary

    High-level overview for leadership: key risks, business impact, and recommended priorities.

  • Technical findings

    Detailed findings with evidence, affected assets, and steps to reproduce.

  • Risk rating

    Consistent risk ratings (e.g. Critical/High/Medium/Low) and, where applicable, CVSS scores.

  • Proof of concept

    PoC or minimal reproduction for critical and high findings to support remediation.

  • Remediation guidance

    Concrete remediation steps and best practices to prevent recurrence.

  • Optional re-test

    Follow-up validation after fixes to confirm findings are resolved.
