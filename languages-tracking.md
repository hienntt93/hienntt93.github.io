🧠 SPEAKING (Band 8 structure)
🎯 Topic: Technology
Câu hỏi:
How does technology affect our life?
Band 8 Answer mẫu:

```

```


```
Technology has fundamentally transformed the way we live, work, and communicate. 
For example, smartphones allow instant access to information and enable global connectivity. 
However, over-reliance on technology may reduce face-to-face interactions and lead to social isolation. 
From my perspective, while technology brings immense benefits, it is crucial to use it in moderation.
```

👉 Bạn phải:

✍️ WRITING TASK 2 (Band 8 sample)
🎯 Topic:
Some people think remote work is beneficial. Others disagree.
Essay mẫu:

```

```


```
Remote work has become increasingly popular in recent years. 
While some argue that it improves flexibility and productivity, others believe it reduces collaboration.

On the one hand, working from home allows employees to save commuting time and maintain a better work-life balance. 
For instance, many workers can manage their schedules more efficiently.

On the other hand, remote work may limit communication and teamwork. 
Without face-to-face interaction, misunderstandings can occur more frequently.

In conclusion, although remote work offers significant advantages, companies should adopt a hybrid model to balance flexibility and collaboration.
```

      *  numbers

      *  transitions (however, but, etc.)
