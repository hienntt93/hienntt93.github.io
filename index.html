Process

Engagements are delivered async/remote. I work in EU timezone and align with your team as needed.

  1. 1 Scoping call (30 min) — Goals, scope, and deliverables.
  2. 2 Access & setup — NDA, accounts, and environment access.
  3. 3 Testing / audit execution — Hands-on assessment.
  4. 4 Report + walkthrough — Findings, prioritization, and Q&A.
  5. 5 Fix support + retest — Remediation guidance and optional re-test.

FAQ

Do you work fully remote?
Yes. All engagements are delivered remotely. I'm based in EU and work in EU timezone.
Can you sign an NDA?
Yes. I'm happy to sign NDAs and data processing terms before any access or data sharing.
What do you need from us to start?
Scoping call, signed NDA (if required), and access to the in-scope systems (e.g. staging API, AWS account with read-only where appropriate). I'll send a short checklist after the call.
How do you handle data access and confidentiality?
Access is limited to what's needed for the engagement. I don't retain your data beyond the report delivery. All work is confidential and covered by NDA where requested.
Do you provide remediation support?
Yes. Findings include step-by-step remediation guidance. Optional re-test after fixes is available to confirm closure.
Can you work with our engineers asynchronously?
Yes. I'm used to async collaboration (tickets, docs, Slack/email). We can schedule short sync calls when needed.
What does a good engagement look like for startups?
Clear scope (e.g. "API + auth" or "single AWS account"), staging/test environment, and a single point of contact. Typically 2–4 weeks from kickoff to report, with a walkthrough call included.
